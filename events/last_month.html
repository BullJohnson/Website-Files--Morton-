Community Events

April 1th - Detector Battery Give-away Event

On Saturday, April 1th, There will be a battery give-away event, at the Morton-Rutledge fire station. Residents with smoke alarms, requiring nine-volt batteries can trade their old batteries for a fresh alarm battery. To receive a new battery, residents must bring an old battery, to exchanage. Only one battery per houshold.

April 18th - Deadling for submitting 2022 tax return

The Morton post office will be open until midnight, to receive your 2022 federal and state tax returns. All return, which are received by midnight, will be post marked for the 18th. Complimentary coffee will be available for borough residents.
