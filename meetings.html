Meetings

The Morton Borough Council meets twice each month. The first is a Public Meeting, held on the second Wednesday, at 7pm, in the borough hall. The second is an Agenda Meeting, which is held on the fourth Wednesday, at 7pm. Although not a public meeting, the Agenda Meeting can be followed via the Zoom link below.

For more information contact Martha Preston, in the Borough Office, at 610-543-4565 or email her at MPreston@MortonPA.org
