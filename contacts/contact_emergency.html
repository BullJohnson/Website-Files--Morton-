Police

Morton's police force is the finest. No one beats them, when it comes to community service and protection. They are the most physically fit and mentally sound officers you will ever have the pleasure of being protected by. Whether it be a cat, stuck in a tree, or a cat-burglar, stuck in your basement window, the Morton PD is there for you.

Their cars are the cleanest and their badges are the shiniest. Relax and enjoy life, knowing that the Morton PD is at the ready. You can always count on your good neighbors, the Morton PD.

In the event of emergencey, don't take matters in to your own hands. Remember to always dial 911.
