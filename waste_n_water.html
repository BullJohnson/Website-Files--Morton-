Residential Curbside Trash Pick-up:

Trash will be collected on Mondays and Thursdays. On holidays, pickup will be on the following Tuesday or Friday. The weight of trash cans should NOT EXCEED 45lbs.

Electronics, propane tanks, compressed gas containers, kerosene heaters, items containing freon and medical waste can not be collected. These items must be recycled at select sites, during the year.

Residential Bulk Pick-up:

Only one bulk item can be collected per week, per household. To make a request for pick-up, contact Republic Services at Phone: 610-265-6337.

Morton Borough has temporarily stopped curbside recycling pick-up, until further notice
